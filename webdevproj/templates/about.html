What is this site about and how did it came to be?

  I took an IT course in college and the final project for the course is to make a functional and usable website. Since I have been playing the game for a very long time already, I thought I'd make a website for this that is not like the wiki but something that might be useful nonetheless. If I am still actively playing the game, chances are, I will keep updating the Ship Tier List (Overall) as they update every month. I will also keep updating the others as long as the original source keeps updating or some kind soul will help me with some data. Even when I have been playing this game since the beginnning of EN, I cannot say that I am very good in the mechanics of the game. I am only here to collect cute ship girls.

  With that, I thank you for taking the time to read this. If you want to help me further improve this, please see the details below.

Where did I get my data?

  The Ship Tier List (Overall) is taking from Usagi-sensei. Tier List No.39 is the source of Ship Tier List (Overall). Credit to u/sapo84 for posting the raw images to the subreddit, u/ragerav and u/chouringjie for translating the tier list.

  The Ship Tier List (Mobs), Ship Tier List (Boss), Recommended PvP Compositions and Recommended General Equipment Loadouts are all from AriPanda's Azure Lane Guide. Their discord is Azur Lane Homeless Shelter. Users are Aristya (Aristya#6666), Panda (DontStandOnTanks#7749) and Doublemint (Doublemint#8851).

Disclaimer: I have not yet asked permission from them to use their data in this website as it is never planned to actually release this to the public, and was only a project to be submitted to my professor. When I ask them permission, and they denied me, I will probably take down a huge portion of this website until another person or group would help me with the data.
