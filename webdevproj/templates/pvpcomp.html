Nagato

Sendai

  Sendai buffs the reload and torpedo (by 3% to 15%) of all allied CL and DD at the same time and has a chance (of 20% to 40% every 20s with the first chance at 10s after the battle starts) to decrease enemy evasion (by 25%) within the skill radius (for 10s).

Position: Left when Jintsu is not in use or Right when Jintsu is in use.

Jintsuu

  Jintsu buffs the reload and torpedo of all allied CL and DD at the same time and increase critical hit chance and critical damage. She also has 20% damage reduction to herself.
